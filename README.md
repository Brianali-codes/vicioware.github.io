## About

> [!NOTE]  
> **This is a Fork of Vicioware.** I do not own the rights to the source code, nor am I an official contributor to the original project. I am simply improving the visuals, making the site a bit more grounded, and planning to add multi-language support.

This website is designed with a single mission: to revive those gems that don't require a powerful GPU to run. If your PC struggles to open two Chrome tabs or you own one of those legendary government-issued laptops, this site is for you.

## Features

The idea is to keep it simple, clean, and functional:

* **Quick Search:** Find your favorite nostalgic games instantly.
* **Smart Catalog:** Explore games alphabetically or filter by the first letter.
* **Direct Downloads:** Forget about link shorteners, countdown timers, or dodging pop-ups.
* **Zero Ads:** A clean experience built by gamers, for gamers.

## Disclaimer

I do not own the copies of the games or the source code of the original website. All games should be purchased from official stores. This project is strictly for **educational purposes**, and I do not support video game piracy.
